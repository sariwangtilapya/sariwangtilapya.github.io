How it works?

There are 5 TILAPYA Levels.

Access is instant if one's level is equal to or greater than the level of the article or exercise.

Articles and exercises at 3 levels higher than one's level and below are accessible. Articles and exercises higher than one's level have a cooldown period. The higher one's level, the faster the cooldown.

Any amount donated with active TILAPYA Access brings the TILAPYA Level to level 1. Every 7 days worth of cumulative active donation increases the TILAPYA Level by 1 up to Level 5. Cumulatively counted from the earliest active donation.

TILAPYA Access works sitewide.

Maximum storeable TILAPYA Access is 100 days. Donations are still active but have to be refreshed.
