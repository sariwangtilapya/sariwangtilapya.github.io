The Object Particle

Nothing else. This particle designates the direct object

But remember this two important points

  1. Direct objects appear only in active voice sentences

    2. The active voice is when the agent (the substantive that does the action) is the subject. Contrary to the passive voice in which the agent is not.

    Active Voice
    The cat ate the fish.
    Passive Voice
    The fish was eaten by the cat.
  2. Certain verbs in English function as adjective in Japanese.
