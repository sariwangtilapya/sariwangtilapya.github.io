The Inclusion Particle

Pronouns

If we add the particle も after an interrogative, it forms a Pronoun of Totality.

But be careful. There is a trap.

Interrogative of TR Pronoun of Tr theoretical Tr practical
何 thing what 何も things included everything nothing
誰 person who 誰も persons included everyone no one
どこ place where どこも places included everywher nowhere
いつ time when いつも times included everytime/always everytime/always

Even though they mean the totality, these pronouns apart from いつも, are only used in negative sentences. Thus consequently, indicates inexistence.
