The Indefinite Particle

more commonly known as the question marker.

And yes, it is true that it is used for such case but the are other uses as well.

Indefinite Pronouns

If we add the particle か after an interrogative, it forms an indefinite pronoun.

Interrogative of Tr Pronoun of Tr
何 thing what 何か indefinite thing something
誰 person who 誰か indefinite person someone
どこ place where どこか indefinite place somewhere
いつ time when いつか indefinite time some day

Disjunction (or)

When presenting more that one choice, か is used, as having more then one possibility is indefinite.

Question Marker

Yes or no, who, what when,… As they are undefined, か is used as well.
