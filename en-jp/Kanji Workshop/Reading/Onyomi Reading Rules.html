Onyomi Reading of Individual Kanji

An onyomi reading can only have two mora at max.
  1. First
  2. Second

The first mora can be any kana*. It could even be a digraph**.

*The kana ぢ,づ,を have been rendered obsolete, replaced by

  • ぢ→じ
  • づ→ず
  • づ→じゅ
  • を→お
**Digraphs in ふ et ゔ not included

The second mora simulates the final consonant. It could be:

  1. う段 kana
    [xxx not included]
  2. ん
    cannot have digraphs as first mora except じゅ
  3. き
    first mora must be い段 or え段 only
  4. ち
    There are only seven kanji having ち as second mora
  5. vowel elongation
  6. diphthong
