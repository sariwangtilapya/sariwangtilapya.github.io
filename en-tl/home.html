Congratulations! You already have attained more than 50% progress in learning Tagalog just by knowing English as Filipino can be spoken with nouns, verbs and adjectives in all English – and some adverbs and disjuctions as well.

This course will teach Tagalog using English vocabulary in Tagalog grammar. Basically the bare minimum Filipino which could be still considered Filipino.
From here, one could start learning without having the lack of vocabulary as a stumbling block.

Although, vocabulary will be provided below. Please use the opportunity to exercise the lesson by reconstructing the sentences with vocabulary provided.

Typically, for nouns, adjectives and verbs one can plug in English words and almost everything else such as particles, pronouns, certain adverbs, conjugation, etc. are non-negotiable.
