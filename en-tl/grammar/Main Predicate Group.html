Main Predicate Group

The Main Predicate Group is the main idea in the predicate

  1. Personal
    Person IN-F EX-F LOC
    I akó kamí¹ ko³ namin¹ sa akin / sa'kin sa amin / sa'min¹
    tayo² natin² sa atin / sa'tin²
    II ka³ kayó mo ninyo / nìyo / nyo sa ìyo / sa'yó sa inyó
    III sìya silá nìya / nya nilá sa kanìyá / sa kanyá sa kanilá

    ³"ka" and "ko" combines into "kitá"

    Person IN-F Tonic EX-F Tonic LOC Tonic
    I akó kamí¹ akin amin¹ nasa akin / nasa'kin nasa amin / nasa'min¹
    tayo² atin² nasa atin / nasa'tin²
    II ikáw kayó iyó inyó nasa ìyo / nasa'yó nasa inyó
    III sìya silá kanìya / kanyá kanilá nasa kanìyá / nasa kanyá nasa kanilá

    ¹1st person plural exclusive
    ²1st person plural inclusive

    • IN-Focus case
      Nominative (Subject)
    • EX-Focus case
      Includes:
      • Accusative Case (Direct Object)
      • Possessive Case
      • Agent Role Case
      • Directional Role Case (if verb of diplacement is used)
      • Instrument Role Case (if la chancla verb is used)
    • LOC case
      Includes:
      • Dative Case (Indirect Object)
      • Ablative / Locative Case
