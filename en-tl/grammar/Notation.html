Notation

!!! Outside of linguistic study Tagalog does not use accent marks !!!

By default, words in Tagalog has a long penultimate syllable.
To cancel this, the final syllable will be notated with a vowel with a left accent [áéíóú]

Long syllables will be notated with a vowel with a long accent [āēīōū]

The glottal stop

The glottal stop symbol looks like this mettre le symbole de coup de glotte ici but due to it not being available on the keyboard, we will type it as ¡ and replace it later with the glottal stop symbol. You might see ¡ from time to time.

This is not standard notation as there is no satandard notation anyway.
