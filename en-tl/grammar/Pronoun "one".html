The pronoun "one"

is the pronoun to reprise when there is only the need to add a desription.
For example:

Could you pass me the ball? The blue one.

I'm gonna buy a lizard - the one that eats cockroach.

There is no pronoun "one" in Tagalog. One just needs to treat the description as a nominal group itself and decline it appropriately.

Pakiabot nga¡ nung bola. Yung blue.

Bībilí ako ng butikí¡ - yung nangangain ng ipis.
