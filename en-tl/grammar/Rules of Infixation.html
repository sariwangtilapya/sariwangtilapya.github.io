Infix

is an affix attached in the word.
Despite this being a stranger, this is as actually the easiest affix to build with. No coalescence, no weird stress rules, no shapeshifting.
There are only two infixes:
  1. um
    only used for Active Voice
  2. in
    used for every other conjugation

Infixes insert themselves after the first consonant phoneme.

  1. pumuntá
  2. pinlano
  3. inaral
  4. shinampoo
  5. inistrike
  2. l or r blends are not a consonant phoneme
  3. empty consonant is considered a consonant phoneme
  4. polygraphs representing the soung of [sh, ch, j, ñ] are considered a consonant phoneme
  5. always the hidden « i » before an s-blend
