Types of nouns

There are two types of nouns :

  1. Proper and Personal (PP)
  2. Everything Else (EE)

The type of noun will determine with which particle must it accord.

Noun particles
Nom si Gen/Acc/Abl si Dat/Loc si Nom pl Gen/Acc/Abl pl Dat/Loc pl
PP Si Ni Kay Silá/Siná Nilá/Niná Kanilá
TR Yung/Ang Ng Sa
Personal Pronouns
Nom Gen/Acc/Abl Dat/Loc Tonic
1st person Akó Ko Akin
2nd person* Ka Mo Iyó Ikaw
3rd person Siyá N(i)yá Kan(i)yá
1st person plural exclusive Kamí Namin Amin
1st person plural inclusive Tayo Natin Atin
2nd person Kayó N(i)yó Inyó
3rd person Silá Nilá Kanilá
*Only the 2nd person singular has a distinct tonic case

Demonstrative Determinants/Pronouns

Near the first person Near the second person Far from both
Itó (I)yán Iyón/Yun

Locative Pronouns

Near the first person Near the second person Far from both
Dito D(i)yán Doón/Dun

If Object or place is near both persons, first person always takes priority.
