Sentence Structure

Base Part

Predicate Subject and Objects Group* (Nouns Only)
Negative Auxiliary Predicate Unit* Main Predicate Unit NO PRONOUNS!!!

Describes Main Predicate Unit. Could be

  • Adjective if MPU is Noun
  • Adverb if MPU is Verb
  • Auxiliary Predicate Unit if MPU is Verb

S&C Groups elements' position are interchangeable

**Certain combinations of Negative and Auxiliary Predicate Unit merge to form a single element

***These affect the positioning of the Insert Group

Insert Group

Always inserted after the first element of the main part
EX pronoun* Enclitic group IN pronoun Attribute connector**

If « ka » is used as a pronoun, it takes the slot of the EX pronoun and places the EX pronoun into the slot of the IN pronoun

Presence of adverb or modal verb activates the attribute connector.
