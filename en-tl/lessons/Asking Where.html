Asking Where

Important vocabulary

  • saán - where
  • dito - here
  • papuntá - direction going to
  • yung - subject marker
  • may - there is

One interesting point when asking the location of something in Tagalog is that you can not just ask "Where is this place?".
One must say "Where in here is this place?"

You are looking for something within the vicinity

For example, you are looking for McDonald's and you know that there is a McDonald's in the area. One asks:

Saán dito yung McDó¡?

You are in a restaurant and you ask a staff where the restroom is

Saán dito yung CR?
