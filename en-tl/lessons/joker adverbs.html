Joker adverbs are adverbs which take a special slot in the sentence structure.

Combinations of joker adverbs are placed in a single group within the sentence.

The combinations themselves have their own ordering and is as follows

    1. pa
      still
      again/more (to resume/to continue)
    2. na
      already
      instead (delimited)
    3. na
  2. lang
    only/just
  3. din/rin
    also/as well
  4. namán
  5. po¡/ho¡
    respect marker
    *both are synonymous, variation merely euphonic
  6. po¡/ho¡
    respect marker
    *both are synonymous, variation merely euphonic
    7. palá
    realisation marker

pa and na in combination with naman changes its own meaning

  • pa namán
  • na namán
  • pa lang vs pa rin
  • na—instead (delimited)
  • na vs na rin
  • naman
