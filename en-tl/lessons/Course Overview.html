Filipino is a three lexica language

Don't be surprised to find english and also spanish words when learning and even when using it in practice.

In fact, we discourage trying to use local tagalog words too much. Natives won't spreak like that like AT ALL. We even encourage questioning your teacher if you have not heard any english words during the instruction as it will not be conducive to learning the language that the natives speak.

ACCEPT THAT FILIPINOS WILL USE FOREIGN WORDS DURING THEIR DISCOURSE.

This is the same situation as in English. English consists of words of French, German and local origin sometimes other borrowed foreign words.

Avoiding the French, German and other foreign lexicon words in favour of local words does not make better English. It just sounds weird. And a lot of times those words don't even exist.

For some reason, unfortunately, a lot of native Filipino speakers have not yet internalised this fact. Even academics.
