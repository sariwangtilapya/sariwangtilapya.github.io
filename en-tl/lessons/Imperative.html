An imperative sentence indicates order.

An order can be addressed to three groups

  1. ka you si.
  2. kayó you pl.
  3. tayo you and [me or us]

Imperative sentence follows the structure

[verb]-[pronoun]

When the order is inviting rather than advisory. Just say the verb in its base form
