When conjugating, one finds oneself needing to moodify the verb with repetition.

The repetition that happens in conjugations does as follows

[First consonant unit]-[First Vowel*]
The repeated syllable is accented except when the verb only has one syllable or the accent is right next to the repeated syllable.
    takbó → tātakbó
    puntá → pūpuntá
    alís → āalís
    aral → mag-aaral
    challenge → ichachallenge
    prank → nampaprank
    blangko → nabablangko
    piano → nagpapiano
    dyaryo → dyadyaryuhin
