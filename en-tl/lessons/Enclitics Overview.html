Enclitics are adverbs or particles which indicate nuances of expression and take a special slot in the sentence structure.

Combinations of enclitics are grouped together within the sentence alongside the pronouns and the connector in some cases.

The combinations themselves have their own ordering and is as follows

*subject to euphonic change

  1. (Timeline point particles)
    1. pa
      still/again/more (to resume/to continue)
    2. na
      already/instead(delimited)
    3. ba
      Well… If you didn't/haven't been
      *Only used in conjuction with naman/
  2. ngâ
    emphatic particle
  3. lang
    only/just
  4. din*
    also/as well
  5. namán
    Indicates Contrast
  6. daw*
    quotative particle
  7. pò/hò*
    respect particle
  8. ba
    question particle
  9. palá
    revelation particle
  10. muna
    interim/meanwhile/for the time being
  11. talagá
    really/veritably
