Nouns as Verbs

In Tagalog, one would VERY FREQUENTLY encounter nouns used as the verb itself

Language

There is no verb for to speak a language. Sometimes salitá¡ is used as a substitute but it just means to say something.

To say speak a language conjugate the language itself

  • magtagalog
  • mag-english
  • magjapanese

Tools

  • magkutsara
  • martīlyuhin
  • ibote

Places

When going to places for leisure or to enjoy oneself, just use the place as a verb

  • magbohól
  • magguimarás
  • magbeach
  • magsine
  • magjollibee

Meal

When going to eat something for a meal (lunch, dinner, breakfast even snacks), just conjugate the meal or snack

  • magsinigáng
  • mag-adobo
  • mag-isda
  • maglugaw

Games

  • magbasketball
  • magchess
  • magtekken
  • magdance dance revolution

Musical intruments

  • magpiano
  • maggitara
  • magflute
  • magviolin

Clothing/Worn articles

  • magtshirt
  • magtsinelas
  • Magsalamin

etc
