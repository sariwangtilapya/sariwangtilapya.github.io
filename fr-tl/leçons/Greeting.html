Greeting

In general, Filipinos don't really greet. Greetings are reserved for formal situations.

Avoid the good morning good evenings you've read once in a textbook. It only ends up sounding sketchy. You don't want to give off an impression that you're trying to borrowing money from your friend or you're recruiting someone to a sketcky MLM.

Instead of learning the greeting phrases, this lesson will teach you how to emulate the functions of greetings without or with minimal greeting.
