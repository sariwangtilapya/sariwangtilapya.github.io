Revenir à la page d'accueil

*Repetition usually is limited to two syllables except when with epithet particle

Repeated personal pronouns

usually accompanied by enclitics lang, din

excludes other persons

  • kami-kami
  • sila-silá
  • kayo-kayó
  • tayo-tayo

Repeated question pronouns

indivualises/pluralises element

  • anu-anó whichever things
  • sinu-sinó whichever persons
  • saan-saán whichever places
  • alin-alín whichever things [from a selection]

Repeated period of time

indivualises element each

  • araw-araw every day
  • linggo-linggó every week
  • buwan-buwán every month
  • taon-taón every year

Repeated relative time

  • kani-kanina [lang] just earlier
  • maya-mayâ a bit later
  • bukas na bukas [din] tomorrow exactly

Repeated units

many a unit

  • tone-tonelada many a ton
  • kilo-kilo many a kilo

Repeated adjectivised nouns

having a lot of something

  • butas-butás having plenty of holes
  • tagpí-tagpî having plenty of patches
  • halu-halò having a lot of mixture components

After MPU ang

  • any adjective in nude form

with epithet particle

when an object accentuates its own characteristic

  • karneng-karné so meaty
  • Triple H na Triple H having mannerisms very similar to that of Triple H's
  • tatay na tatay having characteristics very associable to that of a father's

with epithet particle but

When one finds something⋅one accentuating that characteristic

  • bobong-bobó find someone really dumb
  • tangang-tangá find someone really dumb
  • saráp na saráp find something truly delicious

Uncontrollable urges

  • utút na utót can no longer hold the urge to fart
  • ihing-ihî can no longer hold the urge to urinate
  • taeng-taé can no longer hold the urge to defecate
  • iyák na iyák really needs to cry or can't hold tears

doesn't stop doing something / keeps on doing

  • kain nang kain
  • iyak nang iyak

many times nang is spelt as ng

Futility

  • alarm clock alarm clock ka pa, late ka pa rin naman bukas. You use an alarm clock [despite that], you're still going to be late anyway.
  • diet diet pa 'ko, di naman ako pumāpayát i'm dieting, [but] I'm not getting slimmer anyway.
  • shades shades pa, mukha namang munggo wears shades, still looks like a bean anyway.
